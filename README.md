## Introduction  

Facebook allows users to share updates, photos, and videos with friends and followers.  
This guide provides simple and clear instructions for creating posts using both the **Facebook mobile app** and **desktop website**.  
You will also find troubleshooting tips to help you post smoothly.

---

## Before You Get Started  

Before you create a post:  

- Use a supported device (Android, iOS, or a web browser on your computer).  
- Ensure a stable Internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data).  
- Log in to your Facebook account. If you don’t have one, create it [here](https://www.facebook.com).  

> **Note:** You can post from either the Facebook mobile app or the desktop website.

---

## Creating a Post from the Mobile App  

This section explains how to create and share both **text-only** and **photo/video** posts using the Facebook mobile app on Android or iOS devices.

---

### To Create a Text-Only Post on Mobile  

**Description:**  
Use this option when you want to share only text — such as an update, thought, or announcement — without attaching any media.  

To create,

1. On your mobile phone, open the **Facebook app**.  
2. On the home screen, tap **What’s on your mind?**  
3. In the text box, type your message or update.  
4. Tap **Audience Selector** to choose who can view your post.  
5. *(Optional)* Tag people or add emojis.  
6. Tap **Post** to publish your text post.  

**Conclusion:**  
Your text-only post is now visible to your selected audience. You can edit or delete it later using the post options.

---

### To Create a Photo or Video Post on Mobile  

**Description:**  
Choose this method to share photos or videos directly from your phone’s gallery or camera to your Facebook feed. 

To create,

1. On your mobile device, open the **Facebook application**.  
2. On your **News Feed** or **Profile Page**, tap **What’s on your mind?**  
3. On the post creation screen, tap the **Image** icon.  
4. From your device gallery, select one or more photos or videos to upload.  
5. Tap **Next (Android)** or **Done (iPhone/iPad)** in the top-right corner.  
6. Type a caption or description for your post in the text box.  
7. *(Optional)* Tap **Add more** to include additional media.  
8. Tap **Audience Selector** to choose visibility for your post.  
9. Tap **Post** to share your media content.  

**Conclusion:**  
Your photo or video post is successfully shared on Facebook. Friends and followers can view, react, and comment on your content instantly.

## Creating a Post from a Computer (Web)  

This section explains how to create and publish posts on the Facebook website using a Windows or Mac computer.

---

### To Create a Text-Only Post on Web or Computer  

**Description:**  
Use this method to share a written post, announcement, or message from your desktop browser without attaching any media. 

To create,

1. On your web browser, go to [https://www.facebook.com](https://www.facebook.com) and log in.  
2. On your **News Feed** or **Profile Page**, click **What’s on your mind?**  
3. Type your message or status update in the text box.  
4. Click **Audience Selector** at the top of the box to choose who can view your post.  
5. *(Optional)* Tag friends or add emojis.  
6. Click **Post** at the bottom to publish.  

**Conclusion:**  
Your text-only post is now published and visible on your timeline. You can later edit or delete it from the post menu.

---

### To Create a Photo or Video Post on Web or Computer  

**Description:**  
Select this option to upload and share pictures or videos from your computer’s file system to your Facebook timeline. 

To create,

1. Open your browser and visit [https://www.facebook.com](https://www.facebook.com).  
2. Click inside the **What’s on your mind?** text box on your News Feed or profile.  
3. Below the text box, click **Photo/Video** to upload images or videos.  
4. In the file manager window, select one or more files to upload.  
5. Click **Add Photos/Videos** to include more media if needed.  
6. Type a caption or description in the post text box.  
7. Use **Audience Selector** to set visibility preferences.  
8. Click **Post** to publish your media.  

**Conclusion:**  
Your photo or video post is now successfully uploaded and shared with your chosen audience on Facebook.

## Frequently Asked Questions  

**Q1. Do I need a Facebook account to create a post?**  
Yes. You must log in before creating or publishing any post.  

**Q2. Can I save a post as a draft?**  
Yes. You can save drafts and access them later from the **Posts** section.  

**Q3. Can I edit a post after publishing it?**  
Yes. Click or tap the **three dots (…)** on your post and select **Edit Post**.  

**Q4. How do I delete a post?**  
Open your post, click or tap the **three dots (…)**, and choose **Delete**.  

**Q5. Can I schedule a post from the mobile app?**  
No. Scheduling is available only on **Meta Business Suite** or the desktop version.

## Troubleshooting  

**Issue 1: I can’t find the option to create a post.**  
- Ensure you’re on the **Home** tab.  
- Look for the **What’s on your mind?** box.  
- Update your Facebook app or browser if the option is missing.  

**Issue 2: My post won’t publish.**  
- Check your internet connection.  
- Restart the Facebook app or your browser.  
- Clear cache or cookies.  
- Update to the latest version.  

**Issue 3: The app or browser freezes while posting.**  
- Close and reopen Facebook.  
- Restart your device.  
- Update or reinstall the app.
